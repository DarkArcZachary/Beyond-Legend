Who are we?

DarkArc Games is a small group with 11 developers. We are located on the west coast and create open world games that are dynamic and are for the players, not the money.

Why are the players more important?

In the game industry today, there is so many games that are over-hyped because the developers advertised a false statement and it caused the players to pre-order the game to install it and be hit with anger and a "I want my money back!" attitude. For DarkArc, the players mean more then money I would rather have a community of 1000 people then 100,000 dollars. Because money runs out, support, kindness, and motivation don't.
